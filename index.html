How to Play

Simon shows a growing sequence of colors. Watch carefully, then repeat the same sequence by clicking the buttons in order.

  1. Click "Start Game" or the title to begin.
  2. Watch the flashing color pads and listen to the sounds.
  3. Repeat the sequence by clicking the buttons in the same order.
  4. If you make a mistake, the game ends and you can restart.
